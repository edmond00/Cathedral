# sections

- **Village Square** — open central space, well, foot traffic, communal gathering
- **Craft Row** — workshops clustered together; living quarters above or behind
- **Market End** — miller, baker, brewer at the working end of the village

# areas

Pick 5–8 from this pool (forge and mill always present):
- Square (hub, always present)
- Forge
- Carpenter's Workshop
- Cooper's Workshop
- Weaver's Workshop
- Mill
- Bakery
- Alehouse / Brewery
- Craftsmen Hall (communal living/eating space for workers and masters)
- Sleeping Quarters (upstairs or back rooms attached to workshops — shared or per-workshop)

**Journeyman Weaver** ×0–1 — Workshop morning/afternoon; Craftsmen Hall noon/evening; Sleeping Quarters night

# comments

**RNG rules:**
- Forge and Mill always generate; all others drawn from pool
- Small village (3–4 workshops): forge, mill, bakery, one craft workshop
- Large village (6–8 workshops): full range
- Cooper and Brewer are lowest-priority additions
- Each workshop has 1 master NPC; apprentices/journeymen optional (50% chance each slot)
- Sleeping Quarters shared by all workers in one large room, or one room per workshop (varies)
- Craftsmen Hall always present if village has 3+ NPCs (communal eating/resting space)

**Economy connections:**
- Iron Ore (cave) → Forge → tools distributed to all other locations
- Log/Plank (forest) → Carpenter → Barrel, Beam, Cart Wheel
- Grain (field) → Mill → Flour → Bakery → Bread
- Grain (field) → Brewery → Ale
- Wool (farm) → Weaver → Cloth
- Flax (field) → Weaver → Linen

**Saw economy note:**
Saw is forged here and found in Carpenter's Workshop — a traceable item manufactured in village, used in village.
