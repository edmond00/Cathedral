# sections

- **Lower Slope** — transitioning terrain; forest gives way to open rock
- **Rocky Midslope** — exposed, windy; scree, outcrops, sparse vegetation

# areas

Pick 3–4 from:
- Scree Field (loose rock, unstable footing)
- Rock Ledge (flat shelf with view)
- Alpine Meadow (sheltered hollow, surprising greenery)
- Gorge (narrow cut, stream at base)
- Boulder Field (massive rocks)
- Stream Source (spring or snowmelt origin)
- Slope Forest (dense wood on lower slope)

# spots

**Rock:**
- Rock Outcrop → Stone ×2, Flint, Lichen
- Fallen Rocks → Stone ×2
- Crevice → Flint, Bat Roost (shallow NPC marker)

**Vegetation (sparse):**
- Alpine Herb Patch → Wild Thyme, Wild Mint, Valerian (pick 1–2)
- Lichen Crust → Lichen
- Moss Patch → Moss

**Water:**
- Stream Source → Stone, Clay, Watercress
- Gorge Pool → Stone, Clay

# roads/doors

Paths and connections within the mountain:
- Slope Path: Slope Forest ↔ Scree Field
- Slope Path: Scree Field ↔ Rock Ledge
- Slope Path: Rock Ledge ↔ Boulder Field
- Gorge Passage: Boulder Field ↔ Gorge (narrow, single-file)
- Stream Track: Gorge ↔ Stream Source
- Cave Entrance: Boulder Field → Cave interior (door, if cave present)
- Cliff Ascent: Rock Ledge → Peak (CLIMB_UP / CLIMB_DOWN, high difficulty)

# npc

**Human:** None. Mountain is uninhabited terrain.

**Beast:**
- Mountain Goat (non-hostile, 50%)
- Lynx (hostile, rare — 15%)
- Wolf (hostile, 20%)
- Boar (lower slopes only, 20%)

**Shallow:**
- Eagle (always at least 1 — territorial, mood anchor)
- Marmot (rocky areas, 40%)
- Raven (perching on rock, common)
- Adder (sunny ledges, 25%)

# items

**Stone/mineral:**
- Stone, Flint, Lichen, Moss, Clay

**Herbs:**
- Wild Thyme, Wild Mint, Valerian

**Animal drops:**
- Eagle Feather, Wolf Pelt, Goat Hide, Lynx Pelt (rare)

# comments

**RNG rules:**
- Slope character: sunny southern exposure (Alpine Meadow more likely, more herbs) vs damp northern exposure (gorge, moss, less meadow)
- Gorge present in ~40% of mountains — adds stream and unique navigation
- Alpine Meadow only in sheltered hollows; rarer on exposed peaks
- Boulder Field and Scree Field are the most common areas
- Slope Forest on lower slope if mountain borders a forest location
- Eagle always present — never omit
- Cave entrance connection: 50% chance of containing a door to a Cave location
